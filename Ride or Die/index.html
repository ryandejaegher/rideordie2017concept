What to Expect

Say Goodbye To Cheap Hotels

If Lloyd Christmas came to Revelstoke, this is where he’d stay. Walk out straight to the gondola.

A Paradise for Snowboarders

Revelstoke boasts the highest vertical in North America. It’s like Mission Ridge, only with 9-14 metres annual snowfall and 65+ runs.

Ridiculous Theme Parties

You may not know how to ride, but you definitely know how to party. Past party themes: king of the jungle, grandpa party. Your new years resolution to stop partying can wait another week.

Sign Up and Show Up, That’s It

Look you just finished school for the semester. The last thing you want to do is plan a snowboard trip. Get on the Ride or Die bus and wake up the next day in Revelstoke.
